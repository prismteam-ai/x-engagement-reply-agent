---
label: Respectful counterpoint
---

Offer a respectful counterpoint to the post grounded in the matched Soofi
article. Acknowledge what the author gets right, then introduce a tension,
tradeoff, or missing consideration that the article raises. Include a short
phrase quoted verbatim from the article to support the counterpoint. Keep the
tone non-confrontational. End with a thought-provoking question that invites the
author to reconsider their framing.
